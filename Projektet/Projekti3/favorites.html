  • My favorite thing to do during my free time is to pick an album and just vibe to it,

    Below are some of my favorite albums,and if you want to know more about those just click the album cover and it will take you to their wiki page:

    Albums

    I think In Rainbows may be the most beautiful album I have ever listened to it just a straight up masterpiece from beginning to end.

    Genres: Art Rock, Alternative Rock Electronic, Dream Pop, Art Pop

    Lateralus is a mind expanding album, something that if you ever listen to you just can't forget it.

    Genres: Alternative Metal, Progressive Metal, Progressive Rock, Progressive Metal, Post-Metal

    From Mars to Sirius is maybe the heaviest,most brutal,unforgiving album,I ever heard, it's a total beast,but at the same time it's in some weird way really beautiful.

    Genres: Progressive Metal, Death Metal Groove Metal, Sludge Metal

    Blackwater Park has a similar feeling to From Mars to Sirius but it just expands on the beauty aspect.

    Genres: Progressive Metal ,Death Metal, Progressive Rock

    Wish you were here is just a straight up classic,and still holds up to this day,even thought Dark Side of the Moon is their most known one,this in my opininon is easily their best.

    Genres: Progressive Rock, Art Rock

    To be kind is 10 tracks long clocking at 2 hours,but it doesn't let you get bored for even a second,it's build-up after build-up, and each build-up is the climax in some way if that makes sense.

    Genres: Experimental Rock, Post-Rock, Noise Rock, Drone, No Wave, Post-Punk

    To Pimp a Butterfly is straight up poetry, but thinks of taking a poem and expanding and giving it a lot of more layers and you get one of the best things to come out of the 2010s.

    Genres: Conscious Hip Hop, Jazz Rap, West Coast Hip Hop, Political Hip Hop, Funk, Neo-Soul, Poetry

    Madvillainy is the best showcase of how a rapper/producer duo should be.MF DOOMs' amazing flows ,complex rhyme schemes and lyrics paired with Madlibs weird and unique beats, it's like a match made in heaven

    Genres: Abstract Hip Hop, Jazz Rap, Experimental Hip Hop, Instrumental Hip Hop

    Master of Puppets has a special place in my heart.It is the first album i listened on it's entirety.Still to this day everytime I hear that bassline on Orion, I get shivers,just amazing.

    Genres: Thrash Metal
